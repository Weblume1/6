Precision Solutions.

01. Building & Structural

High-stakes commercial alterations and residential expansions. We handle everything from foundation integrity to the final structural slab, ensuring every modification meets 2026 industrial safety codes.

02. Master Carpentry

Specialized woodwork including industrial roofing, custom decking, and precision joinery. We combine traditional grit with modern CNC-accurate cutting for flawless installations.

03. Industrial Paving

Engineered for heavy-duty traffic. Our paving solutions for warehouses and driveways focus on sub-base compaction and drainage, preventing sinking and ensuring long-term durability.

04. Precision Painting

Using premium Plascon and Dulux industrial coatings. We provide weather-resistant exterior shielding and professional interior finishes for warehouses, offices, and homes.

05. Commercial Plumbing

Full-spectrum water systems, including high-pressure lines and sewer management. We handle everything from emergency leak detection to complete infrastructure overhaul.

06. Certified Electrical

Commercial and residential wiring, fault finding, and compliance certification. We ensure safe, reliable power distribution for complex machinery and modern living spaces.

07. Tiling & Cladding

Expert installation of industrial-grade porcelain, ceramic, and natural stone. Precision leveling and durable grouting for hygiene-critical and high-traffic areas.

08. Welding & Fabrication

On-site metalwork, including safety gates, structural steel repairs, and custom component fabrication. We work with steel, aluminum, and specialized alloys.

09. Waterproofing

Advanced membrane and liquid application systems for flat roofs, basements, and retaining walls. Protecting your assets from Durban’s challenging climate.

10. Rubble Removal

Efficient site clearing and waste management services. We ensure your project site remains clean, safe, and compliant with local environmental regulations.
